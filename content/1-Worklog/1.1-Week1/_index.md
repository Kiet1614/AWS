**Week 1:** 20–26 April 2026

### Week 1 Objectives:

- Get familiar with the FCJ environment, internship rules, and workplace expectations.
- Understand IAM fundamentals (User, Group, Policy, Role); practice access control, MFA, Switch Role, and Least Privilege.
- Learn core Amazon VPC components: Subnet, Internet Gateway, Route Table, Security Group, and Network ACL.
