**Week 2:** 27 April – 3 May 2026

### Week 2 Objectives:

- Learn and practice deploying Amazon EC2 on Windows/Linux, running Node.js applications, and managing access with IAM.
- Configure EC2 connected to Amazon RDS, deploy a full-stack application, and perform database backup/restore.
- Set up Launch Template, Load Balancer, and Auto Scaling Group for traffic distribution and high availability.
