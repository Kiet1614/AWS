### Week 9 Objectives:

- Optimize EC2/RDS costs with Savings Plans and Reserved Instances.

### Week 9 Outcomes:

- Understood and applied Savings Plans, Reserved Instances, and Reserved DB Instances to reduce EC2/RDS costs.
- Used AWS SCT for schema conversion and DMS for data migration with serverless replication, monitoring, and troubleshooting.
- Configured IAM Users/Groups/Roles with IP and time-based Conditions following least privilege.
- Deployed multiple applications (WordPress, PrestaShop, Akaunting) on Lightsail with snapshots and instance scaling.
- Deployed containerized applications on Amazon Lightsail Container Service from public registries and custom Docker images.
